A project's journey, in three phases.

Beacon now organizes a project's full lifecycle into three modules — Due Diligence, Planning & Permitting, and Compliance. A project moves through each in turn, and Beacon tailors the workspace to the phase you're in. Open any project below to pick up where you left off.
