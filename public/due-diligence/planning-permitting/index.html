From findings to filings.

When a project's Due Diligence work is complete, it advances to Planning & Permitting. This is where the permit matrix becomes a schedule, applications are drafted and submitted, AHJ relationships are managed, and the regulatory record is built. The features below are sketched here and will be detailed in a follow-on prototype.

Projects in Planning & Permitting

1
Permit Strategy
Sequence the permits identified in Due Diligence; tag dependencies; size lead times.
2
Applications
Draft submissions per permit; track agency comments and revisions.
3
AHJ Coordination
Log meetings, calls, and commitments with Authorities Having Jurisdiction.
4
Permit Schedule
Gantt-style view of all permits with clear-to-construct milestones.
5
Conditions Capture
Capture every Condition of Approval as it lands — ready to hand to Compliance.

When a project's permits are cleared to construct, the Conditions of Approval captured here flow automatically into Beacon's Compliance module as commitments — the existing data model.
