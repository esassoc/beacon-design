<BcnRequirementReference> — specimen

The reusable component that replaces the dialog's gray Requirement reference box, shown across three requirements with different metadata. The dialog header carries identity, so the block leads with the requirement text in Besley serif, then a calm definition grid. The conditional trio (Species · Season · Construction activity) and Milestone render only when present. Frequency is fixed One-time.

MM-BIO-2 — Biological Resources 10 fields · conditional trio: 3 of 3
Dialog header MM-BIO-2 Pre-Construction Nesting Bird Survey Survey

Prior to any ground-disturbing activities or vegetation removal occurring during the nesting season (February 1 – August 31), a qualified biologist shall conduct a pre-construction survey for nesting raptors and other migratory birds within the project area and a 500-foot buffer. If active nests are identified, the biologist shall establish no-disturbance buffer zones and submit findings to the City prior to commencing work.
